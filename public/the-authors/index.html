The Joy of Clojure

The Authors

The Joy of Clojure was written by Michael Fogus and Chris Houser, two active members of the burgeoning Clojure community and active contributors to the language itself.

Fogus is a member of Clojure/core with experience in distributed simulation, machine vision, and expert systems. He can be found on the Internet at:

  • Twitter
  • Blog
  • Tumblog

Some of Fogus’ Clojure-related side-projects include:

  • Marginalia
  • clache
  • unk
  • Trammel
  • core.unify

Chouser is a key contributor to Clojure who has implemented several of its features. He can be found on the Internet at:

  • Twitter
  • Blog

Some of Chouser’s Clojure-related side-projects include:

  • data.xml
  • data.finger-tree
